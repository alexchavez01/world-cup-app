# FanZone ⚽📱  
**Watch. Connect. Celebrate.**  
Your ultimate World Cup watch party companion — built to bring fans together in real-time.

---

## 📝 Project Description

**FanZone** is a mobile app designed to unite FIFA World Cup fans through social watch parties. Whether you're watching alone or with friends, FanZone makes it easy to host or join both virtual and local gatherings. The app includes features like real-time chat, match notifications, fan polls, and interactive reactions — creating a shared experience for fans around the world. FanZone brings the energy of the stadium into your living room, dorm, or party.
